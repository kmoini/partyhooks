Frequently asked questions

What is a TikTok party hook?

A party hook is the opening line of your TikTok caption or reel — the thing that makes someone stop scrolling. For party reels, the best hooks are POV-style, emotional, or funny moments that guests relate to instantly.

Do I need to be a TikTok creator to use this?

No — PartyHooks is built for people who rarely post. You're going to a wedding or birthday, you want one great reel, and you need something to say. Enter your occasion and get a hook in seconds.

What occasions work with this?

Any celebration — weddings, birthdays, bachelorettes, graduations, anniversaries, baby showers, New Year's Eve, and more. The more specific you are (e.g. "outdoor wedding" or "surprise birthday"), the better the hooks.

How do I add a dance to my reel?

Click "Add a dance to your reel" under any hook — SnapDance's AI puts you in a matching dance video using just a selfie. No filming, no choreography, no skill needed.
